Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Overview

TodayRSS is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. TodayRSS operates entirely on your device:

  • Your RSS feed subscriptions are stored locally on your device
  • Article content is fetched directly from the RSS sources you subscribe to
  • AI summaries are generated on-device using Apple's built-in AI - no data is sent to external servers
  • Your reading history and preferences remain on your device

Third-Party Services

RSS Feeds: When you subscribe to an RSS feed, TodayRSS fetches content directly from that feed's server. The feed provider may have their own privacy policy regarding how they handle requests.

Apple Services: If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is handled by Apple through the App Store. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles payment data.

On-Device AI

TodayRSS uses Apple's on-device AI capabilities (Apple Intelligence) for article summarization. This processing happens entirely on your device:

  • No article content is sent to external servers for summarization
  • Summaries are generated locally using Apple's built-in language models
  • Your reading habits and preferences are never transmitted

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Feed subscriptions and folders
  • Cached articles and summaries
  • App settings and preferences
  • Saved articles

You can delete all app data by uninstalling TodayRSS from your device.

Children's Privacy

TodayRSS does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
