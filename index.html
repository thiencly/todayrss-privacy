How do I add a new RSS feed?

Tap the + button in the top right corner of the source list, then paste the URL of the RSS feed or website you want to follow.

How does AI summarization work?

TodayRSS uses Apple's on-device AI to generate summaries of articles. This happens entirely on your device - no data is sent to external servers. Requires iPhone 16 or newer, or iPad with M-series chip.

Why do I see "Summarization unavailable"?

On-device AI requires compatible hardware (iPhone 16+, iPad with M-series chip) and iOS 26 or later. If you have compatible hardware, try waiting a moment and tapping the summarize button again.

How do I organize my feeds into topics?

Long-press on a feed in the source list, then select "Move to Topic" to organize your feeds into folders.

Does TodayRSS sync across devices?

Currently, TodayRSS stores data locally on your device. iCloud sync is planned for a future update.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore any previous subscriptions.
